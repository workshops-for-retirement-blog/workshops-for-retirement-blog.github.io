## Why Healthcare Planning Matters in Retirement

Healthcare is one of the largest expenses retirees face. With the variety of choices available in the healthcare marketplace, it can be confusing to know which path is best for you. Should you stick with traditional Medicare, explore Medicare Advantage plans, or look into supplemental insurance? Each choice can impact your finances, your peace of mind, and your family’s well-being.

Attending retirement workshops can help you better understand your options, from how to maximize Social Security to navigating Medicare enrollment periods. These retirement seminars ensure you’re not missing out on crucial benefits or making costly mistakes.
