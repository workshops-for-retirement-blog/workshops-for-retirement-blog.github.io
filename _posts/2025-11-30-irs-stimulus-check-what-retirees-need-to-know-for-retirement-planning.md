## How the IRS Stimulus Check Impacts Your Retirement Planning

Receiving an IRS stimulus check can offer a welcome financial boost, but it’s also an opportunity to review your overall retirement planning strategy. Here’s how:

- **Supplementing Income:** Stimulus checks can help cover unexpected expenses or supplement your regular income from Social Security or pensions.
- **Tax Considerations:** While stimulus payments are not taxable, it’s important to understand how they interact with your other retirement income and tax strategies for retirees.
- **Investment Opportunities:** You may consider using the funds to bolster your emergency savings or discuss investment advice for seniors with a local financial advisor.
