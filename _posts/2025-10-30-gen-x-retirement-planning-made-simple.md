Many are balancing the final years of their careers, supporting families, and managing aging parents—all while thinking about what the next chapter will look like. With Social Security, Medicare, and investment options constantly evolving, it’s natural to have questions about the best way forward. That’s where retirement workshops can make all the difference.

#### What You’ll Learn at a Retirement Seminar

Here’s just a taste of what a free retirement workshop can help you tackle:

- **How to maximize Social Security**  
  Learn about timing, spousal benefits, and how your decisions today impact your future income.
- **Investment advice for seniors**  
  Discover the best way to retire with $500k, diversify your portfolio, and minimize risks as you approach retirement.
- **Tax strategies for retirees**  
  Explore ways to keep more of what you’ve earned, including Roth conversions and required minimum distributions.
- **Estate planning**  
  Get the facts on wills, trusts, and protecting your legacy for loved ones.
- **Medicare basics**  
  Understand coverage options and costs so you can plan ahead with confidence.
